About Game

This story began in 2019. A young policeman on a special assignment of the government was supposed to free the hostages from the hands of evil terrorists. Having only a club in his arsenal, one can count with bare hands without fear and with an unperturbed look, he is trying to do the impossible.

Take in your hands the management of this mission, perhaps you will be the first to succeed in rescuing lovely and beautiful girls as hostages. Good luck!

Start playing

How to play

You can play using the mouse/touchpad. Basically, the game is reduced to pressing buttons (answers to questions and the choice of spells).

But if you have only one keyboard, you can play using the "TAB" key (all elements are highlighted for convenience) and "ENTER".
